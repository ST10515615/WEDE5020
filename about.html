We bring lower prices you can save

Over 40 years ago, Shoprite made a promise to bring the lowest prices in quality food and essential home goods to its customers. This promise is the very foundation of our business and has helped to fuel our growth to becomming Africa's largest grocery retailer. Today, with almost 700 stores in South Africa and more then 170 more across Africa, and 25 million loyal shoppers, Shoprite continues to provide communities with a variety of food products, household goods, home appliances and value-added services - at the lowest possible prices.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver low prices in a world-class shopping environment to customers across the African continent and to uplift lives every day by pioneering access to the most affordable goods and services, creating economic opportunity and protecting our planet

Our Vision

Our vision is to be Africa's most profitable omnichannel retailer, to be the consumer's first choice in food retailing in every market operated, and to create a better, more sustainable future through improved access to goods.

Our Story

Shoprite Holdings is the largest supermarket retailer in Africa and has its origins in South Africa. The company was founded in 1979 when a small group of investors purchased eight grocery stores in the Western Cape.
